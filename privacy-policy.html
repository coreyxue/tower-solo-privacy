Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

Tower Solo ("we", "our", or "the game") is a mobile game developed as an independent project. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The game does not require you to create an account or provide any personal details.

Local Storage

Tower Solo saves game progress (such as your current level, coins, and owned skins) locally on your device using your browser's local storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Advertising

Tower Solo uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain device information to serve relevant ads. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Third-Party Services

Other than Google AdMob, the game does not integrate any third-party analytics, tracking, or data collection services.

Children's Privacy

Tower Solo does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
